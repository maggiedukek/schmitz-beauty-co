YOUR ARTIST

Meet Brenna

Brenna is the artist behind Schmitz Beauty Co., working from her chair at Simplicity Salon in Staples, Minnesota, where bold style meets elevated self-care. With a passion for vivid color and precision lash work, she brings both artistry and intention to every service.

Known for her edgy style and welcoming energy, Brenna keeps her chair inclusive, expressive, and unapologetically you. From color and lashes to waxing, facials, and nails, she delivers high-end results with a personalized experience so every client leaves feeling confident, elevated, and completely themselves.
